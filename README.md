### 🌱 About me

A passionate **Artificial Intelligence and Data Science undergraduate** at **Robert Gordon University** (on the pathway to 2026 graduation), currently working as an **Automation Engineer at General Innovations, Sri Lanka** — where I blend programming, automation, and AI‑driven tools to solve real‑world industrial problems. I’m driven by **curiosity about AI systems**, especially how language models, computer vision, and automation pipelines can be stitched together into robust, user‑aware solutions. ### 🚀 What I’m building right now

- **Industrial AI assistants**: Building workflow‑oriented assistants that help engineers debug and interpret PLC‑based systems using **Qwen‑type LLMs** and retrieval‑augmented patterns (RAG‑style).  
- **NLP‑powered chatbots**: Creating document‑ and article‑based Q&A systems using frameworks like **LangChain** and transformer backbones tailored to technical domains.  
- **AI‑style transfer & image tools**: Exploring **image‑style transfer studios** and **Efficient‑Net‑V2**‑based smart‑bin systems that track and classify waste using vision and IoT‑style stacks.  
- **Assistive‑tech experiments**: Working on web‑based tools aimed at helping **visually impaired users** interact with content more easily through structured UI, audio cues, and semantic navigation flows.

These projects live in my pinned repos and are the “current‑state” of how I’m applying AI + Data Science outside of coursework.
